About Us

Welcome to My Shop! We are a small online store offering a simple selection of everyday products, from electronics to home items.

This site was built as a simple example project using plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Product data is loaded from a JSON file, and your cart is saved right in your browser using local storage, so it will still be there even if you refresh the page.

Thanks for stopping by, and happy shopping!
